Private Policy

What is Collected?

When visiting this website, your web browser may automatically send information such as

type of browser you are using, date and time you access the site, your IP address,

and whether you are a new or returning user.

This information is solely used to determine the number of visitors to different sections of the site and to ensure the site is working properly

In no way is this information passed on for advertsing or third party other than Google Analytics, which is only used to monitor website traffic and uptime

Cookies may be used for the purpose of knowing if you are a returning or new user, however these cookies are not required for site functionality.

Additionally, third-party widgets such as YouTube may install cookies depending on their configuration. You are not required to accept any cookies to use this site.


Is There Any Way to Opt Out?

Yes, if you don’t want Analytics to be used in your browser, you can install the Google Analytics browser add-on.

You can also disable cookies in your browser's settings or delete cookies already in your browser


Google Analytics

This website uses a tool called “Google Analytics” to collect information about use of this site. Google Analytics collects information such as how often users visit this site, what pages they visit when they do so, and what other sites they used prior to coming to this site. I use the information I get from Google Analytics only to monitor traffic and to improve this site. Google Analytics collects only the IP address assigned to you on the date you visit this site, rather than your name or other identifying information. I do not combine the information collected through the use of Google Analytics with personally identifiable information. Although Google Analytics plants a cookie on your web browser to identify you as a unique user the next time you visit this site, the cookie cannot be used by anyone but Google. Google’s ability to use and share information collected by Google Analytics about your visits to this site is restricted by the Google Analytics Terms of Use.
